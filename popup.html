🇫🇮 Dual Sub 📺

Learn Finnish by watching YLE Areena & Ruutu with dual subtitles
Watch Finnish content with both Finnish and target language (English, Vietnamese) subtitles displayed simultaneously - the perfect tool for immersive language learning!

  • Free for basic usage, but requires one-time setup
